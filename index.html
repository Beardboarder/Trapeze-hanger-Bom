Hardware rules used by this calculator
  • All nuts, square washers, and lock washers are 1/2".
  • Every hanger has 2 threaded rods.
  • Threaded rod quantities are not included.
  • Top spanner hardware per rod: 1 nut above, 1 square washer above, 1 square washer below, 1 lock washer below, 1 nut below.
  • Middle tier hardware per rod: 1 nut above, 1 lock washer above, 1 square washer above, 1 square washer below, 1 nut below.
  • Bottom hanger hardware per rod: 1 nut above, 1 lock washer above, 1 square washer above, 1 square washer below, 1 nut below.
  • Spanner style uses 2 window clamps per hanger.
  • B750-J12 style uses 1 Eaton B-Line B750-J12 beam clamp per rod, plus 2 nuts and 2 lock washers per beam clamp.
